## Subpage 1 — The team behind

Goal: make the event feel trustworthy and human. This page should answer: “Who is creating this, and why should I trust them with the room?”

Suggested sections:

### Hero

Headline:

> The people behind FoundersVN

Subheadline:

> Built by operators who care about better rooms, better introductions, and conversations that continue after the event.

### Team cards

Create 2–4 profile cards. Each card should include:

- photo/headshot
- name
- role in FoundersVN
- 2–3 sentence bio
- LinkedIn link
- optional social links: website, Facebook, Instagram, X, portfolio

Do not over-polish the bios into corporate language. Keep them specific, warm, and credible.

Suggested bio format:

> [Name] helps shape [area of responsibility]. Their background spans [relevant work], and they are focused on making FoundersVN feel curated, practical, and genuinely useful for founders in Vietnam.

## Subpage 2 — FoundersVN events

Goal: explain the larger event model and make the pilot feel like the first edition of a thoughtful series, not a one-off dinner.

Suggested sections:

### Hero

Headline:

> FoundersVN events

Subheadline:

> A phone-free networking series built around curated rooms, hosted tables, and a digital layer that keeps the logistics quiet.

### Event model

Explain the model in 3–4 simple blocks:

1. Curated room
   - People are selected for relevance, seriousness, and contribution potential — not just industry.
2. Multiple hosted tables
   - The event is not one long table. It is a curated room across multiple smaller tables.
3. Digital layer
   - The app handles profiles, contacts, table flow, and follow-up context.
4. Follow-up loop
   - The directory stays useful after the event so conversations can continue.

### Timeline / event chain

Show a simple timeline:

- Da Nang — Edition 01 — Friday, July 31, 2026
- Saigon — planned August 2026
- Future cities / future editions — to be announced

### Past event recap

Section title:

> Event recap

For now, this can be a single showcase box rather than individual recap pages.

Before Event #1:

> Recap coming after the first Da Nang edition.

After each event, update this area with:

- 3–5 photos
- number of attendees
- 1–2 short quotes
- 3 key moments
- next edition CTA

Do not create separate recap subpages until there is enough content.

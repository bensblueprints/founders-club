The night

How the event runs

1

App access

Everyone's profile is live. People know who's in the room before a single handshake — what they do, what they want.

2

Bonding + intros

Host-led icebreakers and short self-intros so nobody's stranded. The app suggests who each person should meet.

3

Free time & dinner

Find a table, eat, connect. This is where the real value happens — organic, unforced.

Language plan: bilingual (VI + EN) host, and we reach out 1:1 to VN owners not fluent in English to help them prep their short intro and get the most from the night.

The hard part

We have no brand yet — so we borrow credibility

People pay $150 only if they trust it's legit. With no track record, our proof is who's already in the room.

Seed the room

Maddy leverages her network to bring a few high-quality owners in first — they become the face of the event.

Ads → find early believers

Run ads now to surface genuinely interested, high-quality prospects we can feature: "joining us: ___."

Show, don't claim

Real faces, real venue, real date, a money-back guarantee if we cancel. Let the app's quality do the talking.

We do have legal entities — what we lack is a brand. Seed quality solves the trust gap for the pilot.

# Nurturing & Retention Plan

_The event is the spark. The value (and the recurring revenue) is what happens between and after events._
_Owner: Maddy (community/brand) + An (platform)._

## The question we're answering
After a great dinner, what do we want attendees to do next — and how do we keep them talking, engaging, and coming back (and bringing others)?

## The ladder (from one dinner to an ongoing community)
1. **On the night** → they use the app, exchange cards, feel the room's quality.
2. **Immediately after** → they land in a persistent group + keep app access to the directory.
3. **Between events** → light, valuable touchpoints keep the group warm.
4. **Next event** → they re-book (Saigon) and refer a peer.

## Where the community lives (decide)
| Option | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **WhatsApp / Zalo group** | Zero friction, everyone has it; Zalo huge in VN | Noisy, hard to moderate at scale, no structure |
| **Telegram** | Better for larger groups, bots/channels | Less used by some VN locals |
| **Our app becomes the platform** | Owned, on-brand, keeps directory + cross-event info | More to build; needs a reason to open it |
| Hybrid (chat group + app directory) | Best of both for the pilot | Two places to manage |

> **Recommendation for pilot:** a **Zalo/WhatsApp group** for conversation + the **app** as the durable directory and cross-event hub. Revisit "app-as-platform" after we see engagement.

## Cross-event visibility (a specific feature you asked for)
In the app, attendees should see not just who's in *this* room but **who's going to the next events** — e.g. "5 people from your Da Nang dinner are also coming to Saigon." This:
- Creates FOMO / a reason to book the next city.
- Turns a single ticket into an ongoing membership feeling.
- See [Web App Spec](../05-web-app/WEB-APP-SPEC.md) for the feature note.

## Between-event touchpoints (keep it light, high-signal)
- Post-event recap + group photo + full directory link (within 24h)
- "Intro of the week" — spotlight a member/business (also social content)
- Early-bird for the next city to past attendees + a "bring a peer" referral perk
- Occasional useful drop relevant to *all business owners* (marketing/promotion tips, growth, hiring) — matches our positioning

## Success signals to watch
- [ ] % of attendees active in the group after 2 weeks
- [ ] % re-booking for Saigon
- [ ] # of referrals from past attendees
- [ ] NPS / "would you pay again" from the survey

Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

No Refund Policy

All payments made for Founders Vietnam events and memberships are non-refundable. By completing your purchase, you acknowledge and agree that no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

Event Tickets

Due to the exclusive nature of our events and limited capacity, event tickets cannot be refunded once purchased. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Founders Dinner tickets
  • Any other event tickets or experiences

Ticket Transfers

While refunds are not available, you may transfer your ticket to another qualified individual. To arrange a transfer, please contact us at least 48 hours before the event with the details of the new attendee.

Event Cancellations by Founders Vietnam

In the rare event that Founders Vietnam must cancel an event, attendees will be offered the choice of:

  • Full credit towards a future event
  • Transfer to the next available event

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at hello@foundersvietnam.com

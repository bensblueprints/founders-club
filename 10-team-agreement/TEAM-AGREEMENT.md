## 2. Money

### Cost responsibility
- **Ads:** Benji fronts/operates the ad spend. Treated as a **shared cost reimbursed off the top** of revenue — not a personal donation.
- **Venue/app/other:** paid from the shared pot / whoever fronts is reimbursed off the top.
- Any expense over **$___ (set a threshold)** needs group sign-off before spending.

### Revenue split — PROPOSAL
1. Reimburse **all shared costs first** (venue, app, ads, fees, printing).
2. Split **net profit 3 ways, equally** (Maddy / An / Benji).

**Rationale:** Benji's earlier idea (Benji 50% / Maddy+An 50%) gives one person half and the two people doing the heavier lift 25% each. A 33/33/33 split is fairer for a long-term series where Maddy+An carry more, especially into Saigon. → _Discuss & lock tonight._

See the worked example in [Budget](../06-budget-and-finance/BUDGET.md).

## 3. Decision-making
- Day-to-day: each owner decides within their lane.
- Big calls (price, split, going ahead / postponing, spending over threshold): **all three agree.** If stuck, ______ (Maddy as lead?) breaks the tie.
- Decisions get logged in [meeting notes](../07-meeting-notes/) so nothing is "re-litigated."

## 4. How we work together
- **Respect:** disagree on ideas, not people; anyone can flag "this isn't working" without drama.
- **Reliability:** if you own it, you either deliver or raise a blocker early. No silent slips.
- **Transparency:** money, ad numbers, and sign-ups visible to all three.

## 5. Cadence & comms
- **Meeting rhythm (decide):** with a ~3-week sprint to Da Nang, suggest **daily 15-min check-in this week**, then **2×/week** through the event. Post-event → weekly.
- **Timezone:** all Vietnam-based → default **ICT**; pick a fixed slot (e.g. 9pm).
- **Channel:** one group chat for ops (Zalo/Telegram), this folder for docs of record.

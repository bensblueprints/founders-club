Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before applying

Very selective. We accept roughly 40% of applicants. We're not trying to fill seats—we're building a curated community of serious founders. Every applicant is personally reviewed by our team. We look for founders with proven revenue, genuine ambition, and a collaborative mindset. If you're just looking to "network," this isn't for you.

We require a minimum of $10,000/month in revenue. This ensures everyone in the room has skin in the game and real experience to share. We also evaluate your industry, growth trajectory, and what you can contribute to other founders. Revenue alone doesn't guarantee acceptance—we look at the whole picture.

Simple: Apply and Pay. You submit your application with payment to secure your spot. Our team personally reviews every application within 48 hours. If accepted, you're confirmed. If not accepted, you receive a full refund immediately—no questions asked, no hard feelings.

If you're not accepted, your payment is refunded in full immediately. We process refunds through Airwallex, and funds typically return within 5-10 business days. There's zero financial risk to apply. We encourage you to reapply in the future as your business grows.

Payment upfront ensures we only review serious applicants. It also guarantees your spot if accepted—our events fill up fast. Since we offer 100% refunds for non-accepted applications, there's no risk to you. This system respects everyone's time and keeps our community high-quality.

Your ticket includes the meet & greet with hors d'oeuvres and welcome drinks, a full dinner, rapid-fire founder introductions where everyone shares their story, Founder Bingo over dinner, and access to the Members Portal where you can see who's attending and research connections before the event.

Doors open at 6 PM with a meet & greet over hors d'oeuvres and welcome drinks, followed by a 30-minute open mingle. Then all 60 founders give a 1-minute rapid-fire intro—who you are, what you're building, what you need. Dinner follows, with Founder Bingo played across the tables so you meet people you'd never have approached. We wrap at 9 PM.

Our members include SaaS founders, e-commerce operators, agency owners, tech entrepreneurs, and investors—all doing $10K to $1M+ per month. Industries range from AI and fintech to manufacturing and real estate. What unites them is ambition, execution, and a willingness to help other founders succeed.

Once accepted, refunds are available up to 7 days before the event. After that, we've committed to venues and catering. You can transfer your ticket to another qualified founder with admin approval. See our full refund policy for details.

Most networking events let anyone with a ticket walk in. We vet every single person. No salespeople pitching you. No "aspiring entrepreneurs." No business card collectors. Everyone at our table has built something real. The result? Conversations that actually matter and relationships that lead to real opportunities.

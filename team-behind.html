Team

Co-Founder, Marketing & Community

Maddy is a community builder and ecosystem fosterer, connecting founders and operators across Southeast Asia. In 4+ years across SmartDev, Consen.ai, and Techies Lab, she has worked in marketing and product, helping grow a startup community of over 5,000 people.

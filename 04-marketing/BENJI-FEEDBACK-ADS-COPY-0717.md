# FoundersVN — Benji Feedback Copy Batch (17/07)

## What the feedback changes

- The ad must read as a **dinner**, not a generic networking event.
- Lead with the pain of being in the wrong rooms and meeting people who cannot move the business forward.
- Signal a room of serious founders, owners and operators without making unverifiable income claims.
- Test luxury dining photography against simple text-first dark creatives.
- Split English and Vietnamese messaging. Vietnamese copy is developed for local business-owner motivations, not translated line by line.

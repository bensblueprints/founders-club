# FoundersVN 16:9 Production Rules

## Locked Layout Rule

- Logo can stay top-left/top-safe as in the approved sample, outside the text card.
- Headline, support, event details and CTA must be grouped inside a contained center-left card/panel.
- The card must have visible breathing room from the left, top and bottom canvas edges. It must not touch the canvas edges and must not become a full-height sidebar.
- Text card should be no wider than roughly 38-42% of the canvas and no taller than needed for the copy.
- Do not let headline/support stretch into the center-right or right side of the image.
- CTA must sit inside the same narrow block, directly below event details, not near the bottom edge.
- People/networking scene should remain clearly visible on the right or center-right.
- Full-bleed image only. No phone mockup, no outer poster frame.
- Final delivery size: `1920x1080`.

## Creative Rule

- People-first: every 16:9 creative must read as networking within 1 second.
- Use Deep Pine overlay behind the narrow text block, Warm Bone text, and Ember Coral CTA.
- Avoid empty venue, empty tables, chairs, shelves, bottles or decor as the main subject.
- Use `Da Nang` without accents in event line.

## Approved 16:9 Composition Reference

- Use the visual structure of the approved VN-02 sample:
  - Logo top-left, above the card.
  - Rounded/deep pine card starts below the logo, inset from the left edge.
  - Card contains headline, support, divider, event details and CTA.
  - Card floats over the image and ends above the bottom edge.
  - People/dinner scene stays visible to the right of the card.

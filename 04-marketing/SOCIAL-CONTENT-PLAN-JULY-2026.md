# FoundersVN Social Content Plan — July 12 to July 31, 2026

_English-first. Vietnamese can be localized after the direction is approved._

## Event facts

- Event: FoundersVN — Phone-Free Networking for Founders
- Date: Friday, July 31, 2026
- City: Da Nang
- Venue: 4U Lounge
- Seats: 25
- Ticket: $150
- Positioning: an intimate founder dinner, designed for deeper conversations and quietly supported by technology.

## Core copy direction

Do:

- Lead with people, presence, curation, and intimacy.
- Mention technology as a seamless support layer, not the main emotional hook.
- Keep lines sharp and calm.
- Make the dinner feel premium and real.

Avoid:

- “Digital-first” as the opening idea.
- Product-heavy app copy above the fold.
- Generic “visionaries” language.
- Over-selling exclusivity in a cold or elitist way.

## Vietnamese localization direction

Do not translate line-by-line. Keep it natural:

- “intimate founder dinner” → “bữa tối kết nối thân mật cho founder”
- “quietly supported by technology” → “được công nghệ hỗ trợ nhẹ nhàng phía sau”
- “a closer room” → “một căn phòng gần hơn / một không gian gần hơn”
- “better conversations” → “cuộc trò chuyện đi sâu hơn”

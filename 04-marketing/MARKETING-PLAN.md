# Marketing Plan

_Owners: Maddy (message, theme, copy) + Benji (targeting, campaign ops)_

## 1. Positioning — DECIDED: by level, not by industry
**Who it's for:** **Business owners / founders** — targeted by *title and level*, NOT by industry. The whole point is a room of diverse expertise (retail, manufacturing, tech, services…) where cross-industry connections spark opportunities you wouldn't find in an industry silo.

**Why this framing wins:**
- A retail owner and a manufacturing owner have more to offer *each other* than two people in the same niche.
- "Business owner" is a shared identity with universally relevant needs → content and value that always land: **expanding network, marketing & promotion, growth, hiring, partnerships.**
- Keeps the pool wide enough to fill 25 seats in one city without over-narrowing.

**Core promise (draft):** _A curated dinner of business owners across every industry — where you already know who's in the room, and the person who solves your next problem is already there._

**Why now / why pay:** Owners value time and signal. $150 filters for people serious enough to be worth meeting. That's a feature, not a barrier — say it out loud.

**Note on price expectation:** the earlier related event charged ~$100. At **$150 the expectation is higher** — the experience, curation, and app must visibly justify the jump. (See open question on prior pricing in [Master Plan §2](../01-master-plan/MASTER-PLAN.md).)

## 2. The funnel
```
Targeted Ad  →  Event landing page  →  "Apply / Buy ticket"  →  Pay $150  →  Confirmed + onboarded to app
```
Track conversion at every step (see metrics below). The two hardest drops will be **ad→click** and **landing→pay**.

## 3. Messaging angles to test (ad copy)
1. **Exclusivity** — "25 founders. One curated room. Da Nang, [date]."
2. **Anti-awkward** — "Know everyone in the room before you walk in."
3. **ROI of connection** — "The 3 intros that change your year."
4. **Curation/quality** — "Vetted founders only. $150 keeps out tourists."
5. **Scarcity + local** — "Da Nang's first curated founder dinner. 25 seats."

> Maddy to pick the primary theme + name; Benji to spin variants for A/B testing.

## 4. Ad strategy (Benji)
- **Platforms:** Meta (FB/IG) primary; consider LinkedIn for founder targeting; local FB groups & nomad communities organically.
- **Geo:** Da Nang + reasonable radius; layer interests (entrepreneurship, startups, SaaS, coworking, digital nomad).
- **Retargeting:** Anyone who hits the landing page but doesn't pay → retarget with social proof / scarcity / early-bird.
- **Budget:** ad spend is capped at **$20–30** for the pilot (see [Budget](../06-budget-and-finance/BUDGET.md)). With so little spend, the job isn't managing CPA — it's squeezing maximum reach + conversion out of targeting, organic, and seeding. Flag early if $20–30 can't surface enough interest.

## 5. Trust builders (critical — we're an unknown brand at $150)
Because we have no network and no track record yet:
- [ ] Real faces on the landing page (the 3 of you / host).
- [ ] Clear refund guarantee ("full refund if we cancel or you don't find it valuable").
- [ ] Named venue + real date + real photos of the space.
- [ ] "Seed" a few recognizable/credible attendees and feature them ("Joining us: ___").
- [ ] Testimonials — none yet; capture aggressively at Event #1 for Event #2.

## Designer notes

- Logo: use the wavy icon only in most square materials.
- Logo color: warm bone on deep pine; ember coral when used as a small accent.
- Keep the logo small. Let the headline carry the material.
- Use coral as an accent, not a full brand wash.
- Avoid large wordmarks unless the asset is a cover/header.
- If using real photos, blur/tint them into the system so they feel like atmosphere, not stock photography.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our process is simple: Apply and Pay. You submit your application with payment to secure your spot. Our team reviews all applications within 48 hours. If accepted, you're confirmed for the event. If not accepted, you receive a full refund immediately—no questions asked.

If by any chance you are not accepted to Founders Vietnam, your payment will be refunded in full immediately. We process refunds through Airwallex, and funds typically return to your account within 5-10 business days depending on your bank. There's zero risk to apply.

We require a minimum of $10,000/month in revenue to join. This ensures our community is filled with serious, established founders who can both contribute to and benefit from our network. We also look for founders who are genuinely interested in building meaningful connections, not just collecting business cards.

Your ticket includes a full dinner and drinks, structured founder introductions where everyone gets to share their story, and access to the Members Portal where you can see who's attending before the event and research potential connections.

After the dinner, a select group of 30 founders continue networking on a private yacht cruise along the Da Nang coast. This intimate setting—with premium open bar—is where the deepest connections happen. Limited spots available each month.

Once accepted, refunds are available up to 7 days before the event. After that, we've already made commitments to venues and vendors. You can transfer your ticket to another qualified founder with admin approval. See our full refund policy for details.

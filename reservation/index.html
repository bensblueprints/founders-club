FAQ

Questions, answered straight.

Is FoundersVN a paid event?

Yes. Applying is free, but attending FoundersVN requires payment. This page is the confirmation step for people ready to keep their seat.

Which payment method should I use?

Use SePay QR transfer if you have a Vietnamese bank app. Use international card if you need international payment support; that route includes a 5% Airwallex platform fee.

When is my seat confirmed?

Your seat is confirmed after the payment is received and matched to the name, phone number, and transfer content on this reservation.

What is the refund policy?

Tickets are final and non-refundable if you change your plans or do not attend. You may request an approved ticket transfer. If FoundersVN cancels the event without offering a suitable replacement date, your ticket payment will be refunded.
